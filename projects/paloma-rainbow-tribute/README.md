# Paloma Rainbow Tribute

## Status

Initial concept, image/video prompt package, and voiceover direction created.

## Source Idea

A 15-second memorial video honoring Paloma, a beloved deceased pet. Paloma should appear healthy, happy, and full of life, running toward clouds on a rainbow and becoming part of other beloved dogs and known family members.

## Reference Notes

- Paloma is a small white French poodle.
- Visual reference shows curly white fur, soft fluffy ears, dark expressive eyes, small black nose, and a joyful open-mouth expression.
- Desired style: realistic, cinematic, film-like.
- Emotional direction: tender, hopeful, luminous, peaceful; not dark or sad.
