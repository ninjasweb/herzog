# SCRIPT OUTPUT

## 1. Format

cinematic short-form memorial video

## 2. Duration

15 seconds

## 3. Narrative Structure

Healthy memory -> joyful movement -> rainbow ascent -> peaceful reunion.

## 4. Script Timeline

```text
00:00-00:03 - OPENING MEMORY
Close-up of Paloma in a golden meadow at sunrise. She looks healthy, happy, and alert. Her white curly fur catches the warm light. She turns toward a soft glow in the sky.

00:03-00:07 - JOYFUL RUN
Paloma runs forward through the grass with natural energy. Her ears bounce gently, her mouth open in a happy expression. The camera tracks low and close, matching her movement.

00:07-00:11 - RAINBOW ASCENT
A soft rainbow bridge appears ahead, rising naturally into luminous clouds. Paloma runs up the rainbow, free and excited, surrounded by warm light particles.

00:11-00:15 - REUNION
Paloma reaches the clouds. Other beloved dogs and gentle family silhouettes wait in warm light. She joins them, then turns back toward camera with a peaceful joyful look.
```

## 5. Visual Notes

Use realistic cinematic lighting, soft depth of field, warm sunrise backlight, gentle camera movement, natural rainbow color, and cloud forms that feel serene rather than artificial.

## 6. Audio Notes

Soft piano or warm orchestral pad, light airy textures, subtle paw movement through grass, gentle wind, no dramatic sadness.
