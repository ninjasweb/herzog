# CHARACTER DESIGNER OUTPUT

## 1. Character Role

Paloma is the emotional center of the tribute: a beloved companion shown in her healthiest, happiest form, moving from earthly memory into a peaceful, luminous reunion.

## 2. Character DNA

- Name: Paloma.
- Type: small French poodle.
- Emotional tone: joyful, gentle, affectionate, peaceful.
- Motivation: running freely toward warmth, light, reunion, and love.

## 3. Visual Identity

Small white French poodle with curly white fur, rounded fluffy ears, dark expressive eyes, small black nose, gentle face, joyful open-mouth smile, healthy compact body, soft elegant posture.

## 4. Color Palette

- Body: clean warm white fur.
- Eyes/nose: deep black/dark brown.
- Environment: golden sunrise, soft pastel rainbow, luminous white clouds.
- Accent: subtle warm light particles.

## 5. Expression System

Primary expression: happy open-mouth smile with bright eyes.

Secondary expressions: peaceful curiosity, excited running focus, calm affectionate gaze toward camera.

## 6. Action Pose System

- Standing in meadow, looking toward warm sky light.
- Running through golden grass with ears and fur moving naturally.
- Running upward along the rainbow bridge.
- Turning toward camera from the clouds with a joyful, peaceful expression.

## 7. Props & Objects

No required props. Avoid random collars, toys, costumes, or accessories unless specifically requested later.

## 8. Behavior System

Gentle, light, energetic, affectionate. Movement should feel healthy and free, never frantic or distressed.

## 9. Consistency Rules

- Preserve small French poodle body proportions.
- Keep curly white coat and fluffy rounded ears.
- Keep dark expressive eyes and small black nose.
- Keep Paloma healthy, joyful, and peaceful.
- Do not change breed, coat color, or face structure.
- Do not add illness, injury, age decline, or sadness.

## 10. Base Character Prompt

```text
Paloma, a small white French poodle with curly white fur, soft fluffy rounded ears, dark expressive eyes, a small black nose, gentle face, joyful open-mouth smile, healthy compact body, affectionate peaceful energy, realistic fur detail, elegant natural posture.
```

## 11. Negative Prompt

```text
sick dog, injured dog, sad dog, crying, cemetery, grave, dark horror mood, aggressive expression, different breed, brown or black coat, deformed face, extra legs, plastic fur, cartoon proportions
```
