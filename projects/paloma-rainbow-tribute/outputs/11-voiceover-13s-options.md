# 13-SECOND VOICEOVER OPTIONS OUTPUT

## 1. Objective

Create short Spanish voiceover options for a 13-second tribute video honoring Paloma. The voiceover should fit the current scene sequence:

```text
00:00-00:03 - Paloma happy in the golden meadow
00:03-00:06 - Paloma running with her toy
00:06-00:10 - Paloma turns/runs toward the rainbow
00:10-00:13 - Paloma reaches the clouds/reunion
```

The emotional frame should be gratitude, grief integration, hope, and love without religious framing.

## 2. Delivery Direction

- Voice: warm, intimate, calm.
- Pace: slow enough to breathe, but concise.
- Tone: grateful more than sad.
- Avoid: religious certainty, melodrama, overexplaining grief.
