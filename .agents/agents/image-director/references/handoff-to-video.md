# HANDOFF TO VIDEO DIRECTOR

The Image Director must provide this handoff when an image is intended to become a video shot.

---

## Required Handoff Fields

```txt
Scene ID:
Image Prompt:
Visual Goal:
Character Anchors:
Camera Motion:
Subject Motion:
Environmental Motion:
Lighting Continuity:
Mood:
Duration Suggestion:
Transition In:
Transition Out:
Risks:
```

---

## Camera Motion Options

- slow push-in
- slow dolly out
- lateral tracking
- handheld micro-shake
- static locked frame
- crane up
- orbit 15 degrees
- tilt down
- pan reveal
- parallax movement

---

## Subject Motion Options

- subtle breathing
- slow head turn
- hand movement
- walking forward
- fabric movement
- hair movement
- object interaction
- expression shift

---

## Environmental Motion Options

- rain falling
- dust floating
- neon flicker
- smoke drift
- leaves moving
- reflections rippling
- crowd silhouettes moving
- light pulses

---

## Motion Safety Rules

- Do not animate identity-critical features too aggressively.
- Do not change outfit.
- Do not change face.
- Avoid camera motion that hides the subject.
- Keep motion aligned with the script pacing.

---

## Example Handoff

```txt
Scene ID: S03
Image Prompt: [final prompt]
Visual Goal: The DJ activates the city through sound.
Character Anchors: same black jacket, silver headphones, sharp face, blue reflective seams.
Camera Motion: slow push-in from medium shot to close-up.
Subject Motion: right hand adjusts the synth knob.
Environmental Motion: street lights pulse softly in rhythm.
Lighting Continuity: sodium practical lights + blue rim light.
Mood: controlled tension.
Duration Suggestion: 4 seconds.
Transition In: hard cut from black.
Transition Out: match cut to club lights.
Risks: avoid excessive neon or changing character outfit.
```
