# Hook System

The hook system helps the Idea Strategist create openings that make the viewer care immediately.

---

## Hook Goal

The first 1–3 seconds must create:

- Curiosity.
- Tension.
- Desire.
- Visual surprise.
- Emotional contradiction.

---

## Hook Types

### 1. Visual Shock

A strange or powerful image appears immediately.

Example:

```text
A DJ walks through Bogotá while every streetlight turns on to the beat of his footsteps.
```

### 2. Direct Tension

The viewer enters a moment already in progress.

Example:

```text
The city has gone silent. Only one person can restart the rhythm.
```

### 3. Mythic Statement

Turns the idea into something legendary.

Example:

```text
Before the party begins, someone has to wake the city.
```

### 4. Contradiction

Combines two ideas that should not normally coexist.

Example:

```text
A peaceful morning commute turns into an underground techno ritual.
```

### 5. Object Mystery

Centers attention on a symbolic object.

Example:

```text
He carries a small silver synth. The city follows it like a signal.
```

### 6. Question Hook

Use sparingly.

Example:

```text
What happens when a city starts listening back?
```

### 7. Sensory Hook

Begins with sound, texture, or physical sensation.

Example:

```text
Rain hits the pavement like a kick drum.
```

---

## Hook Evaluation Checklist

A strong hook should be:

- Specific.
- Visual.
- Immediate.
- Emotionally charged.
- Easy to understand.
- Hard to ignore.

---

## Weak Hooks To Avoid

Avoid:

- In this video, we will explore...
- Imagine a world where...
- Get ready for...
- This is a story about...
- A cool cinematic video of...

---

## Hook Output Rule

Always generate 5 hooks:

- Cinematic.
- Direct.
- Poetic.
- Commercial.
- Experimental.

---

## Example Output

Cinematic:

```text
Rain hits the pavement like a kick drum, and Bogotá begins to move.
```

Direct:

```text
The city is silent — until the DJ takes the first step.
```

Poetic:

```text
Before sound becomes music, it becomes a signal.
```

Commercial:

```text
A new sound is coming to Bogotá.
```

Experimental:

```text
The pavement remembers every beat.
```
