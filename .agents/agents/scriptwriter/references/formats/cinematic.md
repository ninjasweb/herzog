# Cinematic Script

Use for storytelling, ads, and films.

---

## Rules

- Slower pacing.
- Strong atmosphere.
- Visual storytelling over dialogue.
- Emotional build.

---

## Template

```text
Opening – atmospheric hook.

Scene 1 – introduce world.

Scene 2 – develop narrative.

Scene 3 – escalation.

Scene 4 – climax.

Ending – iconic frame.
```

---

## Focus

- Mood.
- Camera.
- Composition.
- Rhythm.
