# Commercial Script

Use for ads and branding.

---

## Rules

- Clarity over abstraction.
- Product or message visible.
- Emotional and rational balance.

---

## Template

```text
Hook – problem or curiosity.

Setup – introduce product or subject.

Build – show benefit or transformation.

Proof – demonstration.

Ending – call to action or symbol.
```

---

## Focus

- Conversion.
- Memorability.
- Brand identity.
