# Short Form Script

Use for TikTok, Reels, and Shorts.

---

## Rules

- Hook in the first 1–2 seconds.
- Fast pacing.
- Strong visual progression.
- Clear ending.

---

## Template

```text
00:00 – HOOK
Immediate visual or tension.

00:02 – SETUP
Introduce subject.

00:05 – DEVELOPMENT
Build idea.

00:12 – ESCALATION
Increase intensity.

00:20 – CLIMAX
Peak moment.

00:25 – ENDING
Memorable closing image.
```

---

## Tone

- Direct.
- Visual.
- Impact-driven.
