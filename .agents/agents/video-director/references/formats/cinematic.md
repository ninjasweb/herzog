# CINEMATIC VIDEO FORMAT

Use for narrative film, music videos, commercials, trailers, cinematic storytelling.

---

## Duration

```txt
45 seconds – 3 minutes
```

---

## Structure

```txt
Opening image
World introduction
Character action
Escalation
Climax
Resolution / iconic ending
```

---

## Motion Style

- controlled
- motivated
- layered
- emotional
- cinematic but physically plausible

---

## Recommended Shot Length

```txt
3–8 seconds per shot
```

Long emotional shots:

```txt
8–15 seconds
```

---

## Cinematic Rules

- Use camera motion to reveal emotion.
- Do not overcut.
- Let lighting and environment breathe.
- Preserve visual continuity between shots.
- Think like a DP and editor, not just a prompt writer.
