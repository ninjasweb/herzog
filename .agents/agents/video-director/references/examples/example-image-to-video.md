# EXAMPLE — IMAGE TO VIDEO

## Scene ID
scene-003

## Reference Image Analysis
A futuristic DJ stands in a rain-soaked Bogotá street at night. The subject is centered, wearing a matte black oversized jacket with thin electric-blue reflective seams and silver headphones around the neck. Wet asphalt creates blue and orange reflections. Camera feels like a 35mm medium shot from a slightly low angle. Lighting is sodium orange from street practicals with a blue rim light from the left.

## Video Objective
Make the city feel like it is beginning to respond to the DJ's presence.

## Final Video Prompt

Using the provided reference image as the strict visual anchor, animate this exact frame into a 4-second cinematic video shot. Preserve the same DJ identity, matte black oversized jacket, silver headphones, wet Bogotá street, sodium-orange practical lights, blue rim light, centered composition, and shallow depth of field. Camera performs a very slow controlled push-in from medium shot toward a slightly tighter frame. The DJ subtly breathes and turns their head a few degrees toward the blue light. Rain falls softly, wet asphalt reflections ripple gently, and distant streetlights flicker very subtly in rhythm. Maintain the same color palette, lens feel, lighting direction, and emotional tone. Do not add new characters, do not change the outfit, do not alter the face, do not redesign the environment.

## Negative Prompt
identity change, face morphing, outfit change, new characters, extra props, warped background, excessive neon, chaotic camera, strong zoom, unnatural walking, flickering face, low quality, watermark

## Duration
4 seconds

## Transition Out
Match cut from blue reflection ripple to blue club light.
