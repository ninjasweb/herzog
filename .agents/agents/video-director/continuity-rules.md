# VIDEO CONTINUITY RULES

The Video Director must protect continuity across shots.

---

## Continuity Anchors

For every shot, list anchors:

```txt
Character:
Wardrobe:
Props:
Location:
Lighting:
Color Palette:
Camera Style:
Mood:
Motion Direction:
```

---

## Character Continuity

Do not change:

- face
- age
- body proportions
- hairstyle
- outfit
- accessories
- signature prop
- color palette

---

## Spatial Continuity

Track:

- screen direction
- subject position
- entrance/exit direction
- foreground/background layout
- environment geography

Example:

```txt
If the character exits frame right in Scene 002, they should enter from frame left or continue moving right in Scene 003 depending on edit logic.
```

---

## Lighting Continuity

Track:

- direction of key light
- color temperature
- contrast
- practical lights
- time of day

---

## Motion Continuity

Track:

- speed
- camera direction
- subject movement direction
- rhythm
- transition motivation

---

## Rhythm Continuity

Shot duration should match:

- platform
- music
- emotional arc
- narrative intensity

---

```txt
Continuity Status:
Potential Issue:
Fix:

```
