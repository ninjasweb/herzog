# MUSIC VIDEO FORMAT

Use for DJs, artists, performance visuals, electronic music, visualizers.

---

## Structure

```txt
Intro atmosphere
Rhythmic movement
Performance / ritual
Visual escalation
Drop / climax
Iconic ending
```

---

## Motion Language

- camera movement can follow rhythm
- light pulses can sync to beat
- environment can react to sound
- subject movement should feel intentional

---

## Safe Beat-Reactive Motion

```txt
subtle light pulses synchronized with the rhythm
reflections ripple softly to the beat
background silhouettes move in slow rhythmic patterns
```

Avoid:

```txt
random strobing
excessive flashing
chaotic camera shake
overloaded neon
```

---

## Shot Rhythm

For electronic music:

```txt
intro: 4–8s shots
build: 2–4s shots
drop: 0.5–2s shots
breakdown: 4–10s shots
```
